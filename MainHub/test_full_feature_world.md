!CHAPTER! Forces and Motion
Force changes an object's state of motion and can accelerate mass.
!SECTION!
Newton's laws describe relationships among force, mass, and acceleration.
!FLASH! What is Newton's second law? :: Force equals mass times acceleration.
!FLASH! What does inertia describe? :: Resistance to changes in motion.
!QUIZZ! Which quantity combines mass and acceleration? :: Force.
!EXAM! Describe inertia in one sentence. :: Inertia is an object's tendency to maintain its current motion state.
